Our Treatments

Sacred Healing Therapies

Hover over any therapy to explore its benefits and how it can help you heal naturally.

Shirodhara
Warm herbal oils poured in a continuous stream over the forehead to calm the nervous system and restore mental peace.
Mind · Stress · Sleep
  • Relieves anxiety, depression & fatigue
  • Treats insomnia & promotes deep sleep
  • Improves memory & concentration
  • Balances hormones & addresses ADHD
Virechana
A Panchakarma purification therapy that flushes out toxins and excess doshas from the body through controlled therapeutic purgation.
Detox · Digestion · Skin
  • Flushes toxins from GI tract
  • Clears liver congestion & improves metabolism
  • Treats skin disorders like rashes & allergies
  • Manages diabetes & heart conditions
Kati Basti
Medicated oils pooled over the lower back using a black gram flour dam to treat spinal and lumbar disorders.
Back Pain · Spine · Sciatica
  • Relieves lower back pain & stiffness
  • Treats slip disk & spondylosis
  • Improves muscle flexibility & mobility
  • Removes stagnant toxins from injury site
Janu Basti
Warm medicated oil retained over the knee joint to nourish and strengthen the cartilage and treat arthritis.
Knee · Arthritis · Joints
  • Treats knee osteoarthritis
  • Reduces pain & inflammation
  • Strengthens the knee joint
  • Improves joint flexibility
Nasya Karma
Administration of medicated oils and herbal preparations through the nasal pathway — the gateway to the brain — for head and neck disorders.
Sinusitis · Migraine · Neck
  • Treats sinusitis & migraine
  • Relieves cervical spondylosis
  • Clears congestion of the head
  • Improves vision & memory
Takradhara
Medicated buttermilk poured continuously over the forehead or body to cool the system and treat psoriasis and stress disorders.
Psoriasis · Cooling · Stress
  • Treats psoriasis & skin disorders
  • Reduces hypertension & stress
  • Cools the body & calms the mind
  • Balances Pitta dosha
Netra Tarpan
Strengthening the eyes by retaining medicated ghee in a dough ring built around the eye socket to nourish and rejuvenate vision.
Eyes · Vision · Dry Eyes
  • Treats dry eyes & eye strain
  • Improves blurred vision
  • Nourishes the optic nerves
  • Relieves eye fatigue from screens
Raktmokshan
Ancient bloodletting therapy using medicinal leeches to purify blood, remove toxins, and treat chronic skin and blood disorders.
Blood · Skin · Detox
  • Purifies blood & removes toxins
  • Treats chronic skin disorders
  • Reduces inflammatory conditions
  • Improves circulation
